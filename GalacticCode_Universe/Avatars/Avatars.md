# GalacticCode Avatars: Characteristics & Descriptions

## 1. Coder Kid
- **Role:** Genius Programmer
- **Description:** A bright young coder who can hack into any system and solve complex puzzles.
- **Characteristics:**
  - Wears a hoodie with glowing circuit patterns
  - Carries a futuristic laptop
  - Quick thinker and problem solver

## 2. Explorer
- **Role:** Fearless Gamer
- **Description:** A virtual reality adventurer who navigates unknown worlds with skill and courage.
- **Characteristics:**
  - VR headset and neon sneakers
  - Equipped with a game controller
  - Agile and strategic

## 3. Space Adventurer
- **Role:** Cosmic Traveler
- **Description:** Explores galaxies and discovers hidden tech relics.
- **Characteristics:**
  - Futuristic spacesuit with neon accents
  - Jetpack for interstellar travel
  - Brave and curious

## 4. AI Scientist
- **Role:** Innovator of Artificial Intelligence
- **Description:** Creates advanced AI companions and futuristic inventions.
- **Characteristics:**
  - Lab coat with glowing circuits
  - Holographic pet assistant
  - Analytical and inventive

## 5. Girl Tech Artist
- **Role:** Creative Visionary
- **Description:** Designs virtual worlds and futuristic art using cutting-edge tools.
- **Characteristics:**
  - Colourful tech-themed outfit
  - Digital drawing pad
  - Imaginative and expressive
